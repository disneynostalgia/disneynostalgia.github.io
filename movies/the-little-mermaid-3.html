The Little Mermaid III: Ariel's Beginning / 2008 / Rating: 5/5

The Little Mermaid: Ariel's Beginning is a 2008 animated fantasy feature film, and the direct-to-video prequel to Disney's 1989 film The Little Mermaid. Directed by Peggy Holmes, the film's story is set before the events of the 1989 film and the 2000 sequel, where all music has been banned from the underwater kingdom of Atlantica by King Triton, and his youngest daughter Ariel attempts to challenge this law.
